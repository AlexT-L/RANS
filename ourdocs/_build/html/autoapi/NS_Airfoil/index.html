NS_Airfoil

Implements boundary conditions for Navier Stokes based model of flow over an airfoil.
class NS_Airfoil.NS_Airfoil(input)¶

Bases: bin.BoundaryConditioner.BoundaryConditioner

Implements boundary conditions for Navier Stokes based model of flow over an airfoil. A halo is formed around the mesh containing ghost nodes. Two types of boundary conditions are implemented: wall boundaries and far field boundaries
