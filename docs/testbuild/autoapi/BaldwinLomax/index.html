BaldwinLomax.turbulent_viscosity(params, dims)¶

Baldwin-lomax turbulence model: modtur = 2. Calculates turbulent viscosity at the cell faces. Averages to obtain cell center values fully vectorized routine. * Calculates eddy viscosity, vorticity, total velocity, normal distance. Also calculates outer and innner eddy viscosity.
